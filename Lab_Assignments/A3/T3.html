Recipe for Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Cooked bacon strips
  • Tomato slices

Instructions

  1. Lay out the bread slices on a clean surface.
  2. Spread butter or margarine on one side of each bread slice. This will be the outer side of the sandwich when grilled.
  3. Place cheese slices on the non-buttered side of half the bread slices.
  4. Add cooked bacon strips on top of the cheese.
  5. Place tomato slices over the bacon.
  6. If desired, add a thin layer of mayonnaise or mustard to the other side of the bread slices.
  7. Place the non-buttered side of the remaining bread slices on top of the bacon and tomato, creating sandwiches.
  8. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  9. Once the skillet is hot, place the sandwiches on it, buttered side down.
  10. Grill the sandwiches until the bread turns golden brown and the cheese starts to melt. Flip the sandwiches and grill the other side.
  11. Once both sides are golden brown and the cheese is melted, remove the sandwiches from the skillet.
  12. Let the grilled cheese sandwiches with bacon and tomato cool for a minute or two before slicing and serving.
